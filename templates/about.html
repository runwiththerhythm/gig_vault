About GigVault

Your personal vault for live music memories — from sweaty club shows to massive festival weekends. GigVault helps you track, organize, and relive your live music experiences.

Why GigVault?

Every gig tells a story — the bands you saw, the friends you went with, the songs that stuck. Memories fade, and ticket stubs only tell half the tale. GigVault was created out of a love for live music and the desire to never lose those moments again.

Built for regular gig-goers and festival veterans

  • • Your gigs: Add past shows and upcoming events in one place.
  • • Your photos: Upload images that capture the atmosphere and your festival crew.
  • • Setlists & videos: Link to setlist.fm and discover YouTube clips to relive the music.
  • • Your dashboard: A clear view of what you’ve seen and what’s coming up.

It’s more than a log — it’s a living archive where festival energy meets personal storytelling.
